Tracker Behaviour

Privacy Insight

First-party: Requests sent directly to the website you are visiting.

Third-party: Requests sent to external domains such as ads, trackers, or embedded services.

Tracker Categories:
- Ads: Advertising networks that track user activity for targeted ads.
- Tracking: General tracking services that collect browsing behaviour.
- Social Media: Embedded social platforms (e.g. share buttons, feeds).
- Redirect: Services that redirect users, often used in tracking chains.
- Gambling: Domains related to betting platforms, sometimes linked with aggressive tracking.

High-Risk Categories:
- Phishing / Scam / Fraud: Attempt to steal personal or financial information.
- Ransomware / Malware: Associated with malicious software or attacks.
- Abuse: Suspicious or potentially harmful infrastructure.

Connection Security:
- HTTPS = Secure encrypted connection
- HTTP = Insecure (data can be intercepted)

Risk Level:
Calculated based on the number and type of third-party requests.
More tracking or high-risk domains = higher risk level.

Note:
This extension uses external blocklists to classify trackers and assess risk.
